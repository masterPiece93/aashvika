Name & Meaning

Aashvika is a beautiful Hindu girl's name of Indian origin. Its blessed meaning is “Goddess Santoshi Maata” — the goddess of contentment & happiness — bestowing her bearer with divine grace, peace & everlasting joy. 🙏

✨

Rashi & Nakshatra

Aashvika's moon sign is Aries (Mesh / मेष), ruled by the mighty planet Mars (Mangal). Her nakshatra is the radiant Krittika — symbolising brilliance, fiery energy & an indomitable spirit! 🔥

👑

Lucky Number 1

Aashvika's lucky number is 1 — the number of leaders, pioneers & visionaries. She is destined to be confident, fearless & fiercely independent, carving her own path and inspiring all around her. 🌟

💝

Hindi में अर्थ

हिंदी में आश्विका का अर्थ है — “देवी संतोषी माँ”। मेष राशि की यह नन्ही राजकुमारी साहसी, आत्मविश्वासी और सृजनशील होगी। शुभ अंक 1 इसे जन्मजात नेतृत्व गुणों से संपन्न करता है। 🌸
